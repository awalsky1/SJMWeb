Throne of Glass Series

Sarah J. Maas
Throne of Glass is a Fantasy series written by Sarah J. Maas (SJM). It is the first of three series within the Maasverse. It follows the story of a teenage assassin Celaena Sardotheon whose past haunts her. It is a story about found family and learning to trust again. The story features impressively done character development as well as evlolving storyline. It also features strong friendships and to die for romance.

The series features eight books, one of which "The Assassin's Blade" is a grouping of five novellas that SJM released. Readers have a handful of options for what order they can read the books in. The most common being either "Romantic" or "Publication".
